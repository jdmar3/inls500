Assigned reading

  • Smith, L. C. (1981). Citation analysis. Library Trends, 30(1), 83–106. Retrieved from https://www.ideals.illinois.edu/bitstream/handle/2142/7189/librarytrendsv30i1_opt.pdf?sequence=3#page=88

    Read all of this article. It will be useful for your final projects.

  • Haythornthwaite, C. (1996). Social network analysis: An approach and technique for the study of information exchange. Library &Amp; Information Science Research, 18(4), 323–342. http://doi.org/10.1016/S0740-8188(96)90003-1

    Read this or White (2003).

  • White, H. D. (2003). Pathfinder networks and author cocitation analysis: A remapping of paradigmatic information scientists. Journal of the American Society for Information Science. American Society for Information Science, 54(5), 423–434. http://doi.org/10.1002/asi.10228

    Read this or Haythornthwaite (1996).

  • Chang, Y.-W. (2013). The influence of Taylor’s paper, question-negotiation and information-seeking in libraries. Information Processing &Amp; Management, 49(5), 983–994. http://doi.org/10.1016/j.ipm.2013.03.003

    Skim this article. We will talk about it as an example.
